Built binary classifier models using Regression Models, Random Forests, Decision Trees and Gradient Boosting algorithms to predict whether a police stop would result in an arrest.
The final model serves to assist law enforcement agencies to ensure that adequate resources have been allocated effectively and that officers are better prepared for potential outcomes.
The model also seeks to enhance transparency and accountability within law enforcement by providing insights into the factors that influence arrest decisions
